# 解像度ごとの設定

- WVGA
  - Built-in: Default (WVGA800)
- FWVGA
  - Built-in: WVGA854
- QHD
  - Resolution: 640x960
  - Abstracted LCD density: 320
- HD
  - Resolution: 720x1280
  Abstracted LCD density: 320

# キーボード無し端末

- Android 4.0以上
- Hardware Back/Home keys: no
